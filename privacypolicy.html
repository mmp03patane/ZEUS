Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

ZEUS ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the ZEUS platform.

When you use ZEUS and sign in with your Google account, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google profile photo

  • Your Google Reviews data (if you grant access)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Authenticate you securely
  • Connect your Google account to ZEUS
  • Display and manage your business reviews

4. Data Security

We store data in MongoDB Atlas with encryption and industry-standard security. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

5. Your Rights

You can request to:

  • View or delete your data
  • Withdraw Google permissions

Email your request to support@zeusapp.co.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. We'll post updates on this page and change the effective date.

7. Contact
