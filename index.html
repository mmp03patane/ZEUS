How It Works

Zeus is built exclusively for Xero users who want more Google reviews without the manual follow-up.

1. Connect in Minutes

Securely link your Xero account and Google Business Profile to Zeus. It's a quick, one-time setup that takes just a few minutes.

2. Trigger on Payment

Whenever you mark an invoice as 'Paid' in Xero, Zeus instantly detects the change and automatically sends a personalized review request to your customer via SMS.

3. Collect Reviews on Autopilot

The customer receives the SMS, taps the direct link, and is taken straight to your Google review page. It's a frictionless process that makes getting 5-star feedback easy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Zeus get me more Google reviews? ▼

Every time you mark an invoice as Paid in Xero, Zeus automatically detects this change through secure webhooks and sends an SMS with a direct link to your Google review page. This removes all friction and makes it incredibly easy for customers to leave feedback, which dramatically boosts your response rates.

Do I need anything other than Xero? ▼

Yes — Zeus is designed exclusively for Xero users. You will also need an active Google Business Profile and your customers need to have phone numbers stored in your Xero contacts for SMS delivery to work.

What if my customer doesn't have a phone number in Xero? ▼

Zeus will simply skip sending an SMS for that invoice. We recommend ensuring your Xero contacts have phone numbers for the best results. You can always add phone numbers to existing contacts and future invoices will trigger SMS requests.

What if a customer leaves a negative review? ▼

You'll get notified instantly through your Zeus dashboard, so you can respond quickly and professionally on your Google Business Profile. While Zeus focuses on encouraging positive reviews, we believe that responding to all feedback, both good and bad, is crucial for building trust.

Is there a long-term contract? ▼

No. Our service is month-to-month, and you can cancel anytime, no questions asked. We're confident in the value we provide, so we don't believe in lock-in contracts.

How soon will I see results? ▼

Most customers start seeing new reviews within the first week of setting up Zeus. The more invoices you mark as paid, the more review requests are automatically generated, leading to faster results.
